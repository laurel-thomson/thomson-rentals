We lease one, two, and three-bedroom apartments for affordable prices. Our apartments are conveniently located for NWMSU students and are quiet and spacious. We offer coin-operated laundry on-site for our aparments and our townhouses include a washer and dryer inside the unit. We cover water and trash and you only need to pay for electricity. We do not allow dogs, other pets may be considered on a case-by-case basis. The complexes feature 24/7 lit hallways, dead bolts throughout, and off-street parking lit from dusk to dawn.
